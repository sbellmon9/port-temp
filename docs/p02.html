  scale_color_manual(values = c(
    "BLACK" = "black",   
    "HISTORY" = "red",

  labs(title = "Hand-Plotted: Black History Month",

Goal: The goal of this project was to explore the intersection of data visualization and digital art. I wanted to celebrate Black History Month by manually drawing text using a scatter plot. This portfolio piece challenged me to think about data points as spatial coordinates rather than just numbers, plus I wanted to represent my cultural pride using the colors of the Soulaan flag.

Product: I created a custom scatter plot using ggplot2 in R. This is a coordinate-mapped plot where each point was manually placed to spell out the words “BLACK HISTORY MONTH.” I mapped out the points and visualized them on a plot using notebook paper.

Data: The data was manually created by me. There are a total of 112 observations and 3 variables.

Interpretation: The entire process of manually inputting data and drawing it out on paper took longer than I expected. Mapping out the ‘S’ was the most difficult part. Other than that, I found the entire experience to be very enjoyable and a great way to show off my heritage, something I am way down to do. Entering each singular coordinate was actually soothing and made me enjoy the art of coding much more.
